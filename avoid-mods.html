Mods to Avoid

Fores New Idles: Unsupported and dated, new animation mods will not work with this. Use Nemesis instead


Achievements Mods Enabler: Achievements are already enabled by Engine Fixes


HDT-SMP: Completely replaced and superseded Faster HDT-SMP


Breezehome (By Elianora): Has lots of errors and deleted navmeshes that will cause crashing, do not use without this


Most oversized texture mods: 90% of textures in the game should not be sized higher than 4K, lots of smaller objects sit comfortably at 1K or 2K. The only 2 assets that i know benefit from 8K are mountains and dragons since their UV is wrapped around a very big and complex mesh


Parallax texture mods: These might replace meshes that were fixed by other mods and often without any patch available, good normal maps suffice is most situations


A Matter of Time: Broken plugin, contains HITME records. Resave in xEdit for possible fix, support will not be provided


LOD textures and billboards: DynDOLOD 3 generates its own textures for LOD with far better results that will actually match your full textures (Same goes for terrain mods since xLODGen does that)


SSE Fixes: Completely replaced by all the engine fixes included in the guide


Skyrim Project Optimization: Occlusion is already taken care of by Lux


FallrimTools: Cleaning save files with Resaver will only cause issues, there is nothing that needs to be done if you followed the guide properly
