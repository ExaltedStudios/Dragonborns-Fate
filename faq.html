Frequently-Asked Questions

Q: Why are there no texture mods/so few visual mods?

A: The simple answer is because I don't use texture mods. I don't believe they are necessary and I prefer the consistent performance and visual style without them. Maybe it's because I have spent so many hours playing Fallout New Vegas, but I think SSE looks beautiful with the visual mods from the guide. I also have a 144Hz monitor, so not using any visual mods lets me get a consistent ~120 FPS. It is only recommended to add your own mods if you are an experienced modder. If you are an experienced modder and want to add more visual mods, I would recommend looking through The Phoenix Flavour's visual section.

Why does the guide not use DynDOLOD/xLODGen?

As this guide is meant to be very streamlined and simple, I didn't want to include them. They're relatively complex to use, and would be a big source of end-user confusion among unexperienced modders. That being said, if you are an experienced modder and are familiar with thse programs, it is definitely recommended to use.
